Well...It depends

It depends on which one you feel is the right smart phone for you. But you have to weigh out the pros and cons of each phone.

Iphone

It does have virtual assistance(Siri) which is easy access to the internet.

But some say that charging is slow and even though it is the most expensive smart phone

consumers have to pay for storage upgrades depending on their plan.

Despite the negatives you as a consumer have longer battery life and more secure

with the Face ID feature which unlocks your phone faster.

Galaxy

It comes with a pen. It comes with a wireless charger which is great.

It comes with a Water Carbon cooling, which is good if the phone starts overheating.

But as a consumer you have think about is it better to have a phone with no batteries to avoid over-heating.

To keep in mind look at the dangers of overheating.

Is this phone more secure as an iphone? No, but the good thing is that it comes with an wireless charging

hub that Iphone didn't come out with.

Overall

It is up for you the consumer to decided if you want a phone that is more secure with your information and longer battery life

or a phone that tries to avoid over heating and comes with a wireless charge

The decision is yours
